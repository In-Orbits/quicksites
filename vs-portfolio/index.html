VergeSense — Jan 2024 – Present

VergeSense builds ceiling-mounted workplace occupancy sensors and IoT gateways for enterprise space management. Below are the products referenced throughout.

Sensors
L302
Last-gen battery sensor. K210 (Canaan) YOLO, u-blox BMD340 (Nordic nRF52) Wirepas mesh, L91 battery, Carlco camera CIF. PIR → camera → YOLO → Wirepas → gateway → cloud.
L410
Next-gen battery sensor replacing L302. Alif E3 (22nm SOI, GlobalFoundries). Nordic nRF52840 Wirepas. Bison camera module, Hynix sensor, Commonlands lens, 120° FOV. Custom EVE Energy Li/SOCl&sub2; battery pack (G0998-LF, 3x ER14505 + SPC0920A supercap, 3.6V 8.1Ah). 10-year target life. Injection molded skins.
E106
PoE-powered wired sensor. Variscite VAR-SOM-MX8M-Mini (i.MX8M Mini) running YOLO. Higher polling rate, continuous power. Bison camera. Panasonic PAN1770 (nRF52840) Wirepas mesh. PoE via MPS MP8017. USB-C & ethernet.
Gateway
G400-C
Cellular IoT gateway replacing Rigado Cell. E106 platform derivative. Variscite SOM. Quectel EG25-GL CAT4 modem, 2x external antennas, heavy antenna tuning with WNC. Wirepas mesh bridge. USB-C or PoE power.
G400-NC
Non-cellular variant. Depopulated G400-C, no modem/antennas. Ethernet/PoE/802.11 only. Same Wirepas mesh gateway function.
3rd Party
Rigado Cell (EOL)
Third-party IoT gateway, per-unit license. Replaced by G400. Rigado acquired by Walmart.
Xovis EN1
Swiss-designed stereoscopic people counter. PoE-powered, high-frequency doorway counting. Resold by VergeSense for occupancy analytics.
