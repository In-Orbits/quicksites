The Destination

Todos Santos

  • Whale sharks in La Paz — ~1 hour drive north. One of the few places on earth you can swim with them. Day trip worth doing.
  • Town — surf, art galleries, tacos, cold beers. Spend a morning wandering.
  • On property — horseback riding, Almarena Spa (book early, fills up), pool, private beach.
