The Destination

Todos Santos

A small Pacific town where surf culture, art galleries, and farm-to-table food coexist with total remoteness. The Tropic of Cancer runs right through it. The light hits differently here — golden hour lasts two hours. Desert meets ocean. Most people who visit want to stay forever.

On the Property

Stables & Riding

Sunset rides across the dunes. Full-moon beach rides. Or just hanging with the horses. The Villa Santa Cruz stables are a ten-minute walk from your room.

The Town

Todos Santos Town

Art galleries, taco stands, cold beer on warm patios. The kind of town that feels discovered but somehow still secret. Spend a morning wandering — you won’t be bored.

Adventure

Whale Sharks in La Paz

One hour north, La Paz Bay is one of the few places on earth where you can swim with whale sharks. Optional day trip — absolutely worth it.

Caracara & The Green Room

Two restaurants on property, both exceptional. Caracara is the fine-dining flagship — farm-to-table, sourced from the Villa’s own gardens. The Green Room is where you end up after the pool closes, watching the Pacific go dark with a drink in hand.


Also: Almarena Spa — book your treatments early. They fill up.
