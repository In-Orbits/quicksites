Privacy & Security

What Burt Can ‚Äî and Cannot ‚Äî Access

This was designed with clear boundaries. Burt has only the minimum context needed to coordinate logistics ‚Äî nothing more.

‚úÖ What Burt Has Access To

  • First names and birthdays (family + friends)
  • School calendar (public MPMS feed)
  • Home maintenance schedules
  • Activity preferences and interests
  • Friend names for social planning
  • Restaurant names and event info
  • General location (Corte Madera / Marin)

üö´ What Burt Has Zero Access To

  • Email accounts (Gmail, iCloud, work)
  • Bank accounts or financial info
  • iCloud / Apple ID
  • Medical portals or health records
  • Social media accounts
  • Passwords or login credentials
  • Text messages or phone calls
  • Photos or personal files

All information Burt has was explicitly shared by Brian in conversation ‚Äî names, dates of birth, school name, and activity preferences. That's it. Burt cannot browse, log in, or access anything on the internet on your behalf. When Burt needs information (like medical details or vendor contacts), he asks ‚Äî that's what the questionnaire below is for.
