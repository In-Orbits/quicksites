HomeBase

A family operating system designed to reduce cognitive overload, keep both parents in sync, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

The System

How HomeBase Works

One weekly sync. The rest runs on autopilot.

01

Monitor

Burt watches school calendars, deadlines, event sources, and maintenance schedules. Always on.

02

Sunday Brief

A complete week-ahead lands every Sunday morning: schedule, events, deadlines, meals, weekends.

03

Confirm

We review, adjust, and lock the week. Mairin gets it via email — no new apps.

04

Autopilot

Day-of reminders, coordination nudges, and logistics run automatically. The week just flows.

05

Learn

Quick feedback after activities — what worked and why — makes it smarter over time.

Framework

The Planning Horizon

Everything follows the same time-based framework. No more scrambling.

6+ months

Research options, deadlines, and costs. Surface the best picks.

3–6 months

Narrow options. Flag deadlines. Lock registrations.

1–3 months

Confirm, book, and arrange coverage for gaps.

1–4 weeks

Plan is set. Reminders fire. Everything covered.

The Operator

Meet Burt

Your AI Chief of Staff

Burt is an AI assistant Brian set up to run family logistics. Think of him as a tireless personal assistant who never forgets a deadline, always has weekend ideas, and keeps the whole machine running.

Brian talks to Burt via Discord. Mairin gets a weekly email + Google Calendar invites. No new apps required.

Privacy & Security

Clear Boundaries

Burt has only the minimum context needed to coordinate logistics. Nothing more.

What Burt Knows

  • First names and birthdays
  • School calendar (public feed)
  • Home maintenance schedules
  • Activity preferences and interests
  • Friend names for social planning
  • General location (Marin County)

Zero Access To

  • Email accounts (Gmail, iCloud, work)
  • Bank accounts or financial info
  • iCloud / Apple ID
  • Medical portals or health records
  • Social media accounts
  • Passwords, texts, photos, or files

All information Burt has was explicitly shared by Brian in conversation — names, birthdays, school name, activity preferences. That's it. Burt cannot browse the internet on your behalf, log into any accounts, or access anything sensitive. When Burt needs information (like medical details or vendor contacts), he asks. That's what the questionnaire below is for.

One-Time Input

Mairin's Download Session

One sit-down to give Burt what he needs. After this, Burt maintains everything. No ongoing homework.

Landscaper

  1. Who is the landscaper? (Name / company / contact)
  2. Current schedule? (Weekly? Biweekly?)
  3. What do they do? (Mow, blow, trim, plant?)
  4. Payment details (amount, method, cadence)
  5. Upcoming projects or changes?

Window Cleaners

  1. Who does window cleaning?
  2. Current schedule?
  3. Payment details?

Laura (Nanny)

  1. Pay rate?
  2. Fixed weekly or hourly?
  3. Payment method? (Venmo, Zelle, check?)
  4. Payment schedule? (Weekly, biweekly?)
  5. Days and hours?

Plants & Grounds

  1. How many plants (indoor + outdoor)?
  2. Watering schedules?
  3. Seasonal care needs?

Medical / Dental

  1. Pediatrician name and practice?
  2. Dentist name and practice?
  3. Last well-checks for Charlie and Mac?
  4. Last dental visits?
  5. Ongoing treatments or specialists?
  6. Allergies?
  7. Medications or supplements?
  8. Vaccinations up to date?

Emergency Info

  1. Health insurance provider + policy number?
  2. Emergency contacts beyond Brian & Mairin?
  3. Medical alerts for emergencies?
  4. Does Laura have a signed medical authorization?

General

  1. Anything else you track that Brian should know about?
  2. Recurring appointments not yet captured?
  3. How should Burt flag things to you — through Brian, or directly?
