Head Family · Product Requirements · v1.0

HomeBase

A proactive family operating system powered by Burt (AI Chief of Staff) that eliminates cognitive overload, keeps both parents informed, and ensures nothing falls through the cracks. Brian & Mairin have clear sight into every decision. Burt automates research, surfaces options, and runs systems. A weekly cadence keeps the family coordinated. The rest of the week runs on autopilot.

01 — System

The Core Loop

Burt operates as a proactive family assistant — researching, planning, reminding, and coordinating. Brian is the primary interface. Mairin has full visibility via Google Calendar and a weekly email summary.

01

Monitor

Burt watches school calendars, deadlines, event sources, and maintenance schedules continuously.

02

Sunday Brief

Sunday morning: a complete Week Ahead lands in Discord #week-ahead and Mairin's email. Drop-off/pickup plan, school events, deadlines, weekend headlines, and the meal plan.

03

Confirm

Brian & Mairin review, adjust, and lock the week. No new apps — Mairin gets email + calendar invites.

04

Autopilot

Day-of reminders, coordination nudges, and logistics fire automatically. The week just runs.

05

Learn

Post-activity feedback: thumbs up/down + why it worked (not just what). Recommendations get smarter each cycle.

02 — Framework

Rolling Planning Horizon

Every item — camps, vacations, maintenance, weekend plans — follows the same time-based framework. No more scrambling. For vacations, the horizon extends to 12–18 months.

HorizonModeWhat Burt Does
6+ monthsResearchIdentify options, registration windows, costs. Surface to Brian.
3–6 monthsDecisionNarrow options, flag deadlines, lock registrations.
1–3 monthsLogisticsConfirm schedules, arrange coverage, book what's needed.
1–4 weeksExecutionWeekly plan is set, reminders firing, gaps covered.
03 — Communication

Communication Architecture

Clear channels, clear escalation. No ambiguity about where information flows.

Brian

Discord (primary) + Google Calendar. Brian talks to Burt directly — real-time back and forth.

Mairin

Google Calendar invites + weekly email summary from Burt. No new apps required. Future: family dashboard website (scoping required).

Priority Tiers
FYI
Informational → Sunday brief / email. Review when convenient.
Action
Decision or task needed → Discord + email. Within 24 hours.
Urgent
Time-sensitive / emergency → Discord + direct message. Immediate.

06 — Preferences

Living Preference System

Family preferences evolve. The system tracks and adapts — not a static profile, a living one.

Family Preference Profile

Maintained by Burt, continuously updated:

  • Activity preferences by category (outdoor, dining, cultural, sports, social)
  • Seasonal patterns and energy levels
  • Kid-specific interests — what Charlie and Mac are into right now
  • Dining favorites + "want to try" list
  • Friend/family rotation and recency
  • Kid voice: Charlie (and eventually Mac) gets input on weekend plans and vacation ideas

How It Stays Fresh

  • Quarterly check-in — Burt asks 5–6 targeted questions
  • Post-activity feedback — thumbs up/down + why it worked (not just what)
  • Age milestones — Mac → 4 (Sep), Charlie → 6 (Aug) → suggest new activity tiers
  • Seasonal shifts — refresh event sources and recommendations each season

Trend Scouting

  • Monitors Marin/Bay Area family event sources weekly
  • Surfaces creative, outside-the-box ideas based on profile
  • Flags time-sensitive opportunities (registration deadlines, ticket drops)
  • Attuned to trends in the Southern Marin niche
  • Light-touch hints for meeting new families via community events

08 — Security

Clear Boundaries

Burt has only the minimum context needed to coordinate logistics. Nothing more.

What Burt Knows

  • ✓ First names and birthdays
  • ✓ School calendar (public feed)
  • ✓ Home maintenance schedules
  • ✓ Activity preferences and interests
  • ✓ Friend names for social planning
  • ✓ General location (Marin County)

Zero Access To

  • ✕ Email accounts (Gmail, iCloud, work)
  • ✕ Bank accounts or financial info
  • ✕ iCloud / Apple ID
  • ✕ Medical portals or health records
  • ✕ Social media accounts
  • ✕ Passwords, texts, photos, or files

All information Burt has was explicitly shared by Brian in conversation — names, birthdays, school name, activity preferences. Burt cannot browse the internet on your behalf, log into any accounts, or access anything sensitive. When Burt needs information (like medical details or vendor contacts), he asks. That's what the questionnaire below is for.

  2. Current schedule? (Weekly? Biweekly? What day?)

  4. Payment details (amount, method, cadence)
  5. Upcoming projects or changes?

Window Cleaners

  1. Who does window cleaning? (Name / company / contact)
  2. Current schedule/arrangement?
  3. Payment details?

Laura (Nanny)

  1. Pay rate?
