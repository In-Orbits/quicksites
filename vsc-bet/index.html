Villa Santa Cruz — 23 rooms on 20 acres of Baja beachfront

Villa Santa Cruz sits on 20 acres of Pacific beachfront in Todos Santos, BCS. 23 rooftop villas with private hot tubs, two pools, two restaurants (The Green Room + Caracara), horseback stables, and a wellness spa. The land was purchased ~25 years ago when Todos Santos was a sleepy art town nobody had heard of.

The Land Play

They bought the land for pocket change in ~2001. Today, 20 acres of Baja beachfront is worth $8–15M by itself. The land has appreciated 20–50x before accounting for anything they built on it.
